About Me

My name is Gandham Jayanth. i am a computer science student at Mahindra Ecole Centrale. this website is my personal project. Through this website I aim to solve a problem most people have,budget control. i have put a lot of effort in this website and i think most people will find it helpful.

budget control is the biggest financial issue in the present world.One should look after their budget to get ride off their financial crisis. i think my website will help you people to keep track of and control your budget.(the website is php based so any functionality like login and sign-up cannot be done in html but i wanted to present the idea i am working on).

What is the scope of this website

i have worked on this website using php,html,css,and backend database with MySQL but as i couldnt find a way to make my website database server online free of cost,i cannot present the websites best features in html alone.This website allows you to monitor your budget on a daily basis with ease.you can navigate through the website with ease and there will be alerts of unnecessary purchases. this will allow you to monitor and manage your budget without much concern.
